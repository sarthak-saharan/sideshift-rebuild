FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is UGC and why does it matter for my brand? +
UGC (User-Generated Content) is authentic content made by real creators showcasing your product. It consistently outperforms brand-produced content in conversion rates and trust — often by 3–5x. It's the most cost-effective form of performance creative today.
How quickly can I find and hire creators? +
Most brands receive 10–20 qualified applications within 24 hours of posting a campaign. Our matching algorithm surfaces the most relevant creators from our 700K+ network instantly — no cold outreach needed.
How do payments work? +
You set payment terms when creating a campaign. When content is approved, payments are automatically triggered. We support PayPal, ACH, wire transfers, and Stripe. We also handle W-9 collection and 1099 generation at year-end.
What industries does SideShift support? +
SideShift works for brands across all consumer categories: health & wellness, beauty, fashion, food & beverage, tech, home goods, fitness, supplements, pet care, and more. If you sell a product, we have creators for it.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes. You can start with a free 14-day trial — no credit card required. Post your first campaign and start receiving creator applications immediately.
How is creator quality ensured? +
Every creator goes through a verification process including content quality review, engagement rate validation, and identity confirmation. Brands can view portfolio samples and past campaign data before hiring.
Can I track which UGC content is actually driving sales? +
Yes. Our performance dashboard shows real-time metrics including views, CTR, ROAS, and conversion data linked to each piece of content. You can identify your top performers and reallocate budgets instantly.
